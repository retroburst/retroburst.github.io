ShintoMon.Unity.3D

ShintoMon takes much influence from the shrine that stands on the shore of Lake Biwa in Shiga Prefecture, Japan. The game is based on classic breakout games of the 80's, you must grant all the wishes by collecting all the ema at the shrine with bouncing orbs. Ema are wishes written on wooden (and sometimes gold) tablets at Shinto and Buddhist shrines all over Japan. The game environment is an attempt at providing a calming atmosphere and gaming experience. The goal here was not to make the game play very difficult, and in place of challenge a rather relaxing experience.

How to Win

You need to collect all the ema using a bouncing orb, through all levels the game throws at you.

Notes

The game will store your preferences such as mute state of music and sound effects, you can also save and load your game.

JewelMine.Unity.3D

Jewel Mine is a simple game, reminiscent of 'Columns' on the Sega GameGear. It is written as an exercise to flex skills, try my hand at a small game and learn some Unity. It is a port of the original JewelMine game to Unity. This version uses the engine from the original game largely intact.

Default Bindings

  • Movement - Left, Right and Down Arrows or Swipe Left, Right and Down for Touch
  • Swap Jewels - Space or Tap for Touch
  • Pause Game - P
  • Restart Game - R
  • Toggle Music - M
  • Toggle Sound Effects - N
  • Change Game Difficulty - D
  • Save Game - S
  • Load Game - L

How to Win

You win by either clearing all jewels in the mine (which is very hard to do) or by scoring enough points from forming groups of like jewels to beat the last level of the game. To form a group of jewels you must have at least 3 like jewels in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line. Other jewels in the vicinity will also be pulled into the group, but at least 3 must be in a line. You will receive more points for a group based on a diagonal line of 3 or more than vertical or horizontal. As the level increases, some parameters of the game logic are tweaked, most noticeably is the speed.

Number of Levels by Difficulty

  • Easy : 255 levels
  • Moderate : 500 levels
  • Hard : 1000 levels
  • Impossible : 10000 levels

Notes

The game will store your preferences such as mute state of music and sound effects, the game difficulty you last played.
